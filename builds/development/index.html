About BridgePoint Studios

Our Strategy

Our goal is to provide quality mobile-friendly websites and various online solutions using the latest technology available. We, at BridgePoint Studios, believe that Quality and Trust are the essential elements needed to create a website that accurately represents you and your business.

Your Local Solution

BridgePoint Studios is located in Florence, AL and is proud to be part of such a Vibrant and Creative community. Covering Florence, the Shoals area of Northwest Alabama and beyond, BridgePoint Studios wants to help you create the perfect website and provide solutions that assist in growing your business. Your success is our success.

Our Services

Website Design

BridgePoint Studios uses the latest technology to provide fully responsive mobile friendly websites. We work with the client to learn about their business and their marketing ideas. Then we do our homework. We learn as much as we can about the industry, the fads and anything else that help us create the best website possible.

Website Maintenance

A website doesn’t do you any good if it can’t be seen. Our basic hosting package includes all the maintenance necessary to keep your website online. We can also make any changes or add additional features to the website at our low hourly rates. BridgePoint Studios is always available to help!

Website Hosting

BridgePoint Studios provides secured and monitored hosting services. For the yearly or monthly hosting fee, your website will receive regular backups, updates and monitoring to ensure that it's viewability remains intact. We strive for 99.99% uptime.

Search Engine Optimization

Social Media and SEO or Search Engine Optimization are all about increasing your online presence. Social Media platforms such as Facebook and Twitter, to name a few, are excellent ways to integrate your online presence and website. SEO is a method of increasing your ranking in search results. We can assist you with both.

Email Marketing / Design

If you need assistance with an Email Marketing plan, contact us at BridgePoint Studios. We can design and setup a turn-key email marketing plan for your business, or we can execute your email marketing in-house. Let us help you tell everyone about your business today.

Web / Mobile Applications

We can help you put your business in the palm of every consumer's hand. Your app can be listed in the Apple App Store, on Google Play, and the Windows Store. We can also create Web-based or Excel Applications to automate your business processes.
